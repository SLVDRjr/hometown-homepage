Hidden Leaf Village

The hidden village of the Land of Fire. Konoha resides deep within a forest at the base of a mountain known as the Hokage Rock, which has the faces of all those who have taken the office of Hokage engraved on it.

TOP 5 Attractions in the Hidden Leaf Village

Hokage Rock

Envisioned by the first hokage as a symbol that the hokage's would always watch over the village.

Ichiraku Ramen

A small restaurant in Konoha that is runned by Teuchi and his daughter Ayame. This restaurant sells the best ramen in Konoha.

The Hot Spring's

A public bath divided down the middle between genders. A places where you can relax.

The Dango Shop

Famous for their delicious dumplings. The shop also serves other desserts such as Anmitsu.

The Academy

A school for beginner ninja. Students learn the shinobi rules, how to strengthen their mind and bodies, and receive special lectures from veteran shinobi.
